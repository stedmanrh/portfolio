Blue Sky Energy is a unique partnership based on a combined 30 years of entrepreneurial technology, general contracting, and government planning and zoning.


Gary Rochlin / Managing Partner
Gary is an upper level executive who has more than 20 years' success with entrepreneurial companies focused on renewable energy and technology.

  • 2011–Present:  Engaged both in the executive suite and in the field analyzing Blue Sky Energy's New Mexico projects for financial viability, structuring finance and working with operations selecting and awarding sub-contractors and infrastructure venders for project build-out.
  • 2010–Present:  Blue Sky Energy – California, a division of Rochlin Corporation, which is a boutique renewable energy concern working in the design, entitlement, construction and close out of Photovoltaic, Solar Thermal, Micro Hydro and energy efficiency
  • 2012:  Was awarded a Certificate of Commendation from the City of Los Angeles for dedication and contribution to environmentally sustainable projects with in Los Angeles.
  • 2010–2012:  Director of Renewable Energy with Spears Renewable Energy and ReGreen Inc. to target the photovoltaic market for each firms’ construction and ESCO clients.
  • 2008–2010:  COO for PermaCity Construction and VP of Commercial Sales for PermaCity Solar running their small commercial and residential solar division.
  • 2006–2008:  Designed, developed, and built by hand a sustainable “green” residence in Pacific Palisades, CA constructed with materials, finishes, and systems that draw on green building practices and incorporated renewable energy sources to minimize its carbon footprint.
  • 1995–2006:  Founder and President of a SRS Industries, Inc. a $20+ million a year, 54-employee operation at peak of operations. SRS started as a third party supplier for the computer services industry and transformed itself into a leader in the refurbished wireless infrastructure industry.
  • 1988–1995:  Founded a real estate development firm and an environmental assessment and consulting firm which was sold utilizing an employee buyout plan.

Matthew Green / Project Manager

  • 2013:  Project manager Blue Sky Energy
  • 2009–2012:  Project manager Permacity Solar
  • Installed over 700kW of solar since 2009
