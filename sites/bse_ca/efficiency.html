new mexico

In line with our LEED accreditation, we have always advocated making our clients' residence or business more energy efficient prior to the installation of a solar array. Basically picking the low-hanging fruit: we these energy efficient improvements include replacing older HVAC units with higher efficiency modern units, installing or upgrading insulation, changing out window sashes from single pane to dual pane, replacing light bulbs with HEF or LED lights, and installing cool roofs. The solar energy system takes the cake. Once it's in place, you will not only be spending less on electricity, you'll be using less electricity.
