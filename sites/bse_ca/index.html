Blue Sky Energy has been actively involved in the Photovoltaic (PV) Solar Industry since 2006. With our 35-year background in general contracting, telecommunications and real estate development, we have always been on the leading edge of integrating sustainable building and energy efficiency practices that have emerged over the past 20 years into our projects. Our building experience coupled with our knowledge of solar panel installation gives us a thorough understanding of design, incentives, entitlements and the construction process. We have experience installing roof mounts, ground mounts, and installations utilizing solar support structures. In Southern California, Blue Sky Energy additionally offers complete energy efficient upgrades including cool roofs, HVAC, lighting, and controls inclusive of the installation of PV and solar thermal arrays. Blue Sky excels with hard-to-conceptualize and hard-to-build projects, maximizing clients' Internal Rate of Return (IRR) in areas where others struggle to break ground.
