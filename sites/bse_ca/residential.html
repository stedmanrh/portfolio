Using solar energy for your home is a wise investment. On average, utility rates in California have been going up 5% a year. Blue Sky Energy strives to ensure our clients get a 6% or more return on their investment per year. As a homeowner with a solar energy system, you will receive a utility rebate and a 30% federal tax credit. Currently customers who install solar on their home generally recoup their initial in 5 to 12 years depending on who their utility is. Solar energy is also environmentally responsible: during a period of 25 years, a residential system will prevent on average 150 tons of greenhouse gases from being released into the atmosphere. Blue Sky Energy handles all aspects of solar installation, including design, permits, construction, and inspection with the local building department and utility. Our materials have 20-25 year warranties, and we respond quickly in the rare chance there is a problem with the equipment. Our impeccable craftsmanship and attention to every detail is what makes Blue Sky Energy the premier choice for our residential customers.
