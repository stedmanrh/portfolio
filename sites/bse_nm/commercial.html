california

Product knowledge, design, and project management are what set Blue Sky Energy apart when it comes to commercial solar installation.  Our small crew uses only the most modern technology and is selective in the projects we take on in order to provide an outstanding level of attention to each client. Our in-house design services are rendered with an eye for not only with what is technically sound but aesthetically pleasing, fulfilling the client's intent. Our portfolio of high-end installations include a 500 kW system for University of New Mexico in Taos and a 1.16 megawatt system for the Buckman Direct Diversion Project in Santa Fe. Blue Sky Energy's work not only delivers results, but outperforms projections.
