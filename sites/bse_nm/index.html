california

Blue Sky Energy was formed in 2011 based on a vision of capitalizing on the unique opportunities presented by solar power development in Northern New Mexico. Abundant solar radiation, high altitude, knowledgeable workforce and strong public support create vast potential for the addition of solar technologies to more standard energy portfolios. Before Blue Sky Energy, most of the large-scale PV systems in New Mexico were developed by non-local companies seeking to exploit New Mexico's local resources while funneling profits out-of-state. Blue Sky Energy has the advantage of a strong local presence, long-standing relationships with suppliers and local subcontractors, and the expertise to ensure the successful operation of its projects for the next 25 years and beyond.
