california

Purchase Power Agreements, such as those with Kit Carson Electric Cooperative, bring Blue Sky Energy to the leading edge of solar power development in New Mexico.  This partnership helps KCEC achieve its state-mandated Renewable Portfolio Standard. In addition, it sets a powerful example to encourage local participation in the ownership and development of renewable energy projects, stimulating local economic development and the inherent community and social benefits that go along with it.
